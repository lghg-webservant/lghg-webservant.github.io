LGHG ONLINE SUBCOMMITTEE REPORT JULY 12, 2020

The online subcommittee has facilitated a venue for addicts to meet and fellowship using the LGHG format since an online presence was created for us by John W. There have now been almost 400 meetings held online using this format. This presence has been maintained without failing to host any of the scheduled meetings. We have created positions and written guidelines to make this possible.

As elections for LGHG are coming up in August it is now time for us to suggest some changes and a greater participation by the home group in general. There are only a few of us trying to carry out many administrative tasks and we need your help.

Here are the titles of the service positions and our recommendation for incorporation to the home group at large ( A detailed description of each service position is below)

Meeting Secretary- it is our recommendation that current meeting secretaries are permitted to continue in their service position if they wish. It is our feeling that changing all these position at once could lead to disruption of meetings.

Attendance Verification-we suggest that the LGHG GC elect someone to this service position.

Web Servant – We suggest that the LGHG GC elect someone to this service position assuming that they meet at least the guidelines set out below.

Service Coordinator-we suggest that the LGHG GC elect someone to this service position to coordinate secretary commitments.

Host/cohost-we recommend that the subcommittee continue to provide and maintain this task for now.

Sub committee member- we are all grateful for the opportunity to serve and wish to continue in our current capacities.

Thank you for allowing us to be of service.

The online subcommittee




LGHG ONLINE SERVICE POSITIONS



ONLINE SUBCOMMITTEE (6 positions)

  • Attend weekly subcommittee meetings if needed
  • Participate in discussions related to online meetings
  • Vote on concerns
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings

SERVICE POSITION COORDINATOR (2 positions)

  • Manage and track all service positions for all meetings
  • Maintain service positions spreadsheet
  • Find substitute if a servant is unable to make a meeting
  • Recruit new servants (hosts, secretaries) if a position becomes vacant
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend LGHG meetings

MEETING SECRETARY (19 positions)

  • Show up 10 minutes early
  • Assign readers
  • Start meeting promptly
  • Follow secretary script to run meeting, call on readers and sharers
  • Ensure meetings follow the LGHG guidelines and secretary procecures
  • End meeting promptly
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings

MEETING HOST (9 positions max)

  • Start meeting 15 minutes early for fellowship
  • Monitor the meeting waiting room (soon the waiting room will be mandatory)
  • Assign Zoom cohost role to secretary (and cohost if needed)
  • Unmute and mute readers and sharers during meeting
  • Lower hands after share
  • Be ready to mute user video if inappropriate content (eg vaping)
  • Be ready to remove a user if they are disruptive or offensive
  • Be ready to respond to “bombers”
  • Rename the secretary on Zoom if they forget: "First L -- LGHG"
  • Keep meeting open for fellowship
  • Capture chat transcript with attendance sheet validation requests, email to attendance validator
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings, access to, and proficiency with, a computer

MEETING COHOST (variable number as many meetings don't need cohosts)

Several hosts prefer to take care of both host and cohost role.

  • Display meeting readings, JFT meditation, Book Study, Serenity Prayer, Third Step Prayer, “Keep Coming Back”
  • Time each speaker and play chime after the allotted time
  • Send chat announcements to chat room during meeting
  • Monitor chat room for messages that need a response, and respond as necessary
  • Inform the host immediately if inappropriate content is displayed
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings, access to, and proficiency with, a computer

ATTENDANCE SHEET VALIDATOR (1 position)

  • Receive chat transcript from host by email
  • Scan for messages in the following format:
Hi, I'm NAME. I attended the online meeting on DATE at TIME. I need an
attendance slip for this meeting. My email address is EMAIL ADDRESS
  • For each such message, send an email from the NA in Place Attendance Email (nainplace.attendance@gmail.com) to the attendee. Use the following template:
To: EMAIL
Subject: Attendance Slip

This email confirms that NAME attended the 6am meeting of Narcotics
Anonymous hosted by Los Gatos Home Group and NA in Place on Tuesday March 31, 2020.
This was a one hour meeting.  For more information see http://lghg.org/

Sincerely,

Linda P.
Host Assistant
  • Update the attendance tracking document with each validation that was sent
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings, access to, and proficiency with, a computer

WEB SERVANT (1 position)

  • Maintain http://lghg.org/ website
  • Ensure lghg.org domain name is active
  • Keep secretary script up to date (add announcements, edit content as needed)
  • Prerequisites: 6 months clean time, regularly attend online LGHG meetings, access to, and proficiency with, a computer
